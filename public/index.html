X-Cell Spreadsheet

Click a cell (it will become blue) to enter a value into that cell by typing the value into the green formula bar at the top of the page. The sum of all numeric values in a given column will be displayed in the pink sum bar at the bottom of the page.

Click a column header (capital letters, top row) to highlight that column. Click a row label (numbers, leftmost column) to highlight that row.

Click "ADD COLUMN" to add a column to the bottom of the spreadsheet (if no column is currently highlighted) or to add a column directly to the right of a currently highlighted column. Click "ADD ROW" to add a row to the bottom of the spreadsheet (if no row is currently highlighted) or to add a row directly below a currently highlighted row.

You can compute the sum of a vertical range of cells by typing a sum in the form "=SUM([col][rowStart]:[col][RowEnd])", for example, "=SUM(A1:A5)", into the formula bar.

*** Be sure to click the "COMPUTE SUM FORMULA" button after typing your sum to display the sum in the currently clicked cell. ***
